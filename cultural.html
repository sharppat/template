Executive Summary

This paper will detail the evident changes in society that stem exclusively from the desensitizing of the pornography industry and its effects on society. You may believe that you have been unaffected by the pornography industry, but you have. Embedded in everyday life is the essence of the industry its self. You will read and learn how the easy accessibility, the influence over media and technology, and the encouragement in the eyes of the teenage population all contribute to the overwhelming impact of the pornography industry upon our culture.
